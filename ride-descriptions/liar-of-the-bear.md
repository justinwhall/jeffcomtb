# Liar of the Bear

Start Location: [https://jeffcomtb.club/directions/liar-of-the-bear](https://jeffcomtb.club/directions/liar-of-the-bear)

Standard Route: 14.5 miles 1990 ft climbing 1990 ft descending.

“Great trail! Fun and flowy with something for everyone”. Mostly a nontechnical ride with the occasional rocky section thrown in here and there. A few challenges throughout the ride should keep even an advanced rider entertained. The route will include (JCOS) newest bike-only, directional trail: Rutabaga, which officially opened May 25, 2023. Rutabaga is a 3.5-mile bike-optimized trail that adds additional climbing and descending to our regular route.
