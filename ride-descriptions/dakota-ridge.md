# Zorro/Dakota Ridge/Matthews Winters

Start Location: [https://jeffcomtb.club/directions/dakota-ridge](https://jeffcomtb.club/directions/dakota-ridge)

Standard Route: 10-12 miles 1700 feet climbing and descending

We will climb the Zorro trail and connect it with Dakota Ridge and Matthews Winters. This is not a beginner level ride. If you are up for a challenge and have good bike handling skills you will enjoy all that this trail has to offer. If this one is to advanced for your skills please check out one of the future rides as the level of difficulty varies from week to week. Due to the size of the crew we usually get for the first ride, and the different pace preferences within the group, there will be a split start. Those that prefer a more moderate pace should be prepared to leave the lot at 5:45 sharp. Those that like to go faster will usually leave the lot 10-15 minutes later than the first group. If you are not sure where you will fit within the group plan on the early start.
