# Virginia Canyon Mountain Bike Park

We park at the Shelly Quinn ballfields [here](https://maps.app.goo.gl/eBe5a6RFEvMFVodm6). Link to directions above. For other parking options see: [Park Idaho Springs](https://www.parkidahosprings.com/).

Trek Trails at Virginia Canyon Mountain Park blends the rich mining history of Idaho Springs with modern-day outdoor recreation. Come and experience a wealth of trails with varying terrain suited for both mountain bikers and hikers. Nestled within the rugged region that was once the epicenter of rich gold mining in the late 1800s, the park represents the future of the town, realized with a new vision of heritage tourism that will bring visitors from around the globe.

The park covers 400 acres and is located just north of town, above the iconic Argo Mine and Mill. Its terrain is steep and rugged, providing great opportunities for challenging rides and hikes. Currently the park has approximately 10 miles of combined multi-use, hiker-only, and bike-only directional trail options to make the most of the terrain for experienced users. Most of the trails are rated blue and black, with some double-black technical trail features on the more advanced lines. Additional future trail expansions in the park are in the planning process.
