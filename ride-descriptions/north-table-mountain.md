# North Table Mountain

Start Location: [https://jeffcomtb.club/directions/north-table-mountain](https://jeffcomtb.club/directions/north-table-mountain)

Standard Route: 12 miles with 1501 feet climbing and descending.

This is a great ride for beginners and experienced riders alike. The trail is a mix of singletrack and doubletrack with some technical sections. We often do a a few laps at the bike park at the end of the ride.


Park info: https://www.jeffco.us/1218/Elk-Meadow-Park
