# Apex Open Space

Start Location: [https://jeffcomtb.club/directions/apex](https://jeffcomtb.club/directions/apex)

Standard loop stats: 11.1 miles with 2400 feet of climbing and descending

“Rocky technical fun. If you have a positive attitude and want a challenge this ride can help improve your skills.”

We will connect most of the loops in the park as conditions allow.
This is a fun technical trail that will make your lungs and legs burn at any point in the season. Some sections always provide a challenge no matter when or how many times you ride them. Due to the size of the crew and the different pace preferences within the group, there will be a split start. Those that prefer a more moderate pace should be prepared to leave the lot at 5:30 sharp. Those that like to go faster will usually leave the lot 10-15 minutes later than the first group. If you are not sure where you will fit within the group plan on the early start.

Directions to parking lot: From Denver travel West on I-70 and exit at Morrison Rd(US 40) Go right at the bottom of the off ramp and travel North for a little over 1 mile pass the Heritage square entrance and turn Left onto Heritage Rd toward Golden and then take an immediate left into the Apex Park East Access.
