# Golden Gate Mule Deer

Standard Route: 11.25 miles 1560 ft climbing 1560 ft descending

Good technical skills are required for this ride. We will head out of the lot and combine a few trails to make a good loop

I recommend carpooling to this ride because there is a $10 entry fee per car to enter the park.

Meet at the " Big Daddy Bagels" parking lot (106 N Rubey Dr, Golden, CO 80403) off HWY 93 in Golden and we will get everyone stuffed into cars. We actually park in the center island area between the bagel shop and the shops to the east. Once we are loaded we will head toward the Kriley's Pond trailhead and start our ride from there. Bring along some money for the entrance fee and meet at the carpool spot at 5:20. Of course you are welcome to skip the carpool shenanigans and head directly to the trailhead if that will work better for you. Hopefully we will be at the trailhead lot ready to ride by 6
